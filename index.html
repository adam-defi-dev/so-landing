About us

Stratton Oakmont

Revives

We named our platform Stratton Oakmont, brokerage firm that was founded in the 1990s. Although that firm was notorious for its unscrupulous practices, we believe that the story of Stratton Oakmont also highlights the need for change and innovation in the financial industry. Our goal is to use blockchain technology and decentralized finance to create a better and fairer financial system for everyone.

What is SO token

The Stratton Oakmont token (STO) is the native currency of the Stratton Oakmont DeFi platform. It is designed to provide a range of benefits to users of the platform, including reduced fees, access to premium features, and the ability to earn rewards through staking.
